  • CRIMSON COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY

    Crimson College of Technology (CCT) has been established in 2000 AD by a group of dedicated academicians from different educational backgrounds like IT, management, professionals from health-sciences and engineers. The college is currently running, Bachelor in Pharmacy (B.PHARM), BBA, BCA and BCIS affiliated to Pokhara University (PU).

  • CRIMSON HOSPITAL

    Crimson Hospital is a registered 50 bedded hospital with multidisciplinary facilities including 10 bedded ICU at Manigram, Rupandehi. The government Hospital Register no is 11/068/069. This hospital is run by Crimson College of Technology affiliated to Pokhara University. Hospital is running at its own building located at Manigram,Rupandehi.
